LIVE FLAGSHIP · PYTHON · QUANT FINANCE

Maritime Quant Dashboard

A live quantitative research app for the shipping sector. It connects freight rate proxies, bunker fuel costs and four listed shipping equities (Frontline, Maersk, Hafnia, Stolt-Nielsen) through a stationarity aware pipeline: rolling correlations, lead-lag cross correlation, and multi-horizon predictive regressions with Newey-West robust errors.

It also ships a walk-forward strategy simulator: a momentum engine and an ML pairs reversion engine that retrains a rolling regression every single day, with signals lagged one day so there is zero look-ahead bias. The lead-lag analyzer was validated against synthetic data with a planted 2 day signal, which it recovered exactly.
